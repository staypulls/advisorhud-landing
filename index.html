The Operational Reality

Multi-Custodian RIA Ops
Is Harder Than It Should Be.

Independent RIAs run complex operations across multiple custodians, manage advisor transitions, and handle high volumes of service requests — without a system built for any of it.

01
✕ Visibility Gap
No Single View of What's Active

Onboarding status lives in emails and memory. Requests are scattered across inboxes. There's no reliable way to know what's open, what's stuck, or what's at risk — without interrupting your team to ask.

02
✕ Ownership Ambiguity
Accountability Breaks Down at Scale

Tasks get assigned verbally or in passing. Without a system of record, ownership is assumed rather than confirmed — and things fall through the cracks on the days that matter most.

03
✕ Reactive Operations
Problems Surface After the Fact

By the time an overdue item becomes visible, it's already an advisor escalation or a client impact. Your team is putting out fires instead of preventing them.

04
✕ Advisor Experience Gap
Advisors Are Left in the Dark

The advisors you recruit and onboard don't know where they are in the process, what's expected of them, or what your team is handling. That uncertainty costs you relationships and referrals.

Platform Capabilities

One System.
Every Operation.

AdvisorHUD was designed around the actual workflow of a multi-custodian RIA ops team — not adapted from generic project management software. Every module reflects how high-performing advisory firms actually operate.

Command Center
AWACS Firm-Wide Dashboard

Every ops team member opens to a live command view — active onboardings, open requests, alerts, workload distribution, and SLA risk. No status meetings required.

Onboarding
Phase-Gated Advisor Onboarding

Full onboarding workflow from initial recruitment through 90-day post-active. Phases unlock progressively as milestones are hit. Advisor sees exactly what's relevant, nothing more.

Requests
Centralized Request Queue

Every service request surfaces in a single prioritized queue with SLA age, assigned owner, current status, and embedded playbooks for complex multi-step workflows.

Intelligence
AI-Powered Morning Briefings

Both ops leadership and individual advisors receive AI-generated daily briefings — personalized, contextual, and built from live mission data. Not a report. A conversation.

Team
Ops Workload Intelligence

Live view of what each team member has open. See where load is concentrated, where capacity exists, and where bottlenecks are forming — before they become problems.

Projects
Internal Initiatives Tracker

Firm-wide projects with milestones, owners, categories, and status visibility. Keeps leadership aligned on internal priorities alongside client-facing operations.

Why AdvisorHUD Exists

Built on a Simple Premise.
Ops Deserves Better Tooling.

AdvisorHUD started as a question: what would RIA operations look like if the team had the same situational awareness a pilot has in the cockpit — everything critical, visible, surfaced before it becomes a problem.

Principle 01
Visibility Before Action

You can't manage what you can't see. Before anything else, AdvisorHUD is designed to give operations a complete, accurate picture of everything in motion — at any moment, without asking anyone.

Principle 02
Exceptions Should Find You

A good ops system doesn't wait to be checked. It surfaces overdue items, SLA risk, and blocked tasks proactively — before they require escalation or become advisor-visible problems.

Principle 03
Ownership Must Be Unambiguous

Every task, every request, every open item has a named owner and a clear status. Not assumed. Not implied. Explicitly assigned and tracked — because accountability is built into the system, not left to culture.
