The Problem

RIA Ops Runs on Spreadsheets,
Emails & Guesswork.

Your team is managing dozens of moving parts across multiple custodians — with no single place to see what's happening, what's overdue, or who's responsible.

✕ Visibility Gap
No One Knows What's Actually Open

Onboarding status lives in emails. Service requests get buried in inboxes. Deadlines slip because nobody has a clear picture of what's active.

✕ Ownership Confusion
Unclear Who Owns What

Tasks get assigned in meetings and lost by Monday. Without clear ownership and status, your ops team is always playing catch-up.

✕ Exception Blindness
Overdue Items Surface Too Late

By the time you realize something is overdue, it's already a problem for the advisor — or worse, for the client.

✕ Custodian Fragmentation
Four Custodians, Four Different Portals

Fidelity, Schwab, Pershing, AssetMark — your team is context-switching constantly with no unified workflow layer on top.

The Solution

One Command Interface.
Total Operational Control.

AdvisorHUD was built specifically for RIA ops teams — not adapted from generic project management software. Every feature is designed around how your back-office actually works.

Onboarding
End-to-End Onboarding Workflows

Track every advisor onboarding from week 1 registration through full platform access. Progress, tasks, deadlines, and blockers — all visible at a glance.

Requests
Centralized Request Queue

Every service request — account openings, transfers, paperwork — surfaces in a single prioritized queue with SLA age, ownership, and status.

Alerts
Exception & Alert System

Overdue tasks, blocked items, and SLA breaches are surfaced automatically. Your team stops items before they become advisor-visible problems.

Team
Ops Workload Visibility

See at a glance what each team member has open. Balance workload, spot bottlenecks, and prevent burnout before it happens.

Custodians
Multi-Custodian Architecture

Purpose-built for firms on Fidelity/NFS, Schwab, Pershing, AssetMark, and Cambridge/WealthPort — with workflows that mirror custodian-specific processes.

Materials
Branded Advisor Materials

Generate and manage branded onboarding materials, checklists, and communications — all inside the same platform your ops team already lives in.

How It Works

Ops That Runs Like a Control Room.

01
Firm-Wide Dashboard on Login

Every ops team member opens AdvisorHUD to a live command view — active onboardings, open requests, alerts, and team workload in one screen.

02
Exceptions Surface Automatically

Overdue tasks and SLA breaches are flagged without anyone having to check. The system brings problems to the team — not the other way around.

03
Clear Ownership at Every Step

Every task and request has an owner, a status, and an age. Nothing falls through the cracks because nothing is ambiguous.

04
Advisors See What They Should

Internal ops notes stay internal. Advisor-facing updates are clean, professional, and appropriate — without requiring a separate system.
